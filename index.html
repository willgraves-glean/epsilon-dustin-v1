What is Glean

Work AI that works across your enterprise

Glean is a work AI platform that combines enterprise search, an AI assistant, and reusable agents on top of a deep enterprise context layer — securely connecting to 100+ systems to index documents, data, people, and processes with full permissions.

Employees use Glean to find and understand information, create and summarize content, and automate workflows, all grounded in live enterprise data and auditable citations.

Tailored value for Epsilon

1. Strengthen the CORE ID & Lotame backbone without increasing risk

  • Give identity, data platform, and privacy teams a single, permission‑aware search and AI assistant across design docs, runbooks, incident reports, DPIAs, and processing terms, so decisions are reusable instead of rediscovered.
  • Shorten onboarding for engineers and data scientists working on CORE ID, Lotame integration, and PeopleCloud by packaging role‑based Collections and self‑serve Q&A on schemas, match logic, and privacy rules.
  • Expose a governed work‑graph retrieval layer to AI Brain and PublicisGPT, avoiding bespoke RAG infrastructure for each internal AI project.

2. Make retail & commerce media execution repeatable globally

  • Give commercial and ops teams a 360° internal view of each retailer or brand (contracts, playbooks, measurement readouts, bugs, and roadmaps) to cut unbilled prep time for pitches and QBRs.
  • Standardize execution with reusable Collections for retail media playbooks by vertical, region, and format, reducing variance across EMEA/APAC and North America.
  • Power an internal “retail media copilot” that can answer questions like “What have we done for a similar grocer in France?” grounded in verified internal docs.

3. Accelerate first‑party data and journey consulting

  • Act as an internal research assistant that automatically surfaces similar client programs, roadmaps, and outcome stories for PeopleCloud and first‑party data initiatives.
  • Help strategy and consulting teams synthesize best practices by vertical and region, keeping recommendations consistent across agencies and markets.
  • Provide a governed knowledge layer that PublicisGPT and Epsilon AI tools can call into so internal guidance on data, consent, and journeys is accurate and aligned to policy.

4. Support safe, efficient AI‑driven transformation across Publicis

  • Equip privacy, legal, security, and governance teams with admin‑level search and sensitive‑content reporting across key repositories to detect overshared PII or contractual artefacts before connecting more systems to AI.
  • Offer employees a single horizontal AI assistant and search experience rather than fragmented point bots per agency or tool, reducing gen‑AI sprawl and confusion.
  • Use curated Collections and announcements by region, agency, and role to streamline rollout of new AI policies, standards, and enablement content.

Why Glean — Differentiators

Governance & security

  • Permissions‑aware by design: Glean respects source‑system ACLs across 100+ connectors, with real‑time sync and identity/SSO integration.
  • Protect+ AI security: Sensitive‑content detection and remediation, support for DLP providers, and controls for prompt injection, jailbreak, and toxic content.
  • Enterprise deployment choices: Run Glean as SaaS or in a dedicated private‑cloud environment, aligning with Publicis’ security and regulatory posture.
  • Centralized guardrails: Admin‑managed policies, audit‑ready logs, and governance for agents and assistant usage across the enterprise.

Deep enterprise context

  • Enterprise Graph that understands data, apps, people, projects, and processes — not just files and folders.
  • Unified indexing of structured and unstructured data so AI can answer questions and take actions in “systems of record” as well as content stores.
  • Independent evaluations show Glean’s answers are preferred significantly more often than generic “company knowledge” features in general‑purpose LLM tools, especially on multi‑system, multi‑step questions.
  • Battle‑tested RAG and relevance at global scale with large, regulated enterprises.

Openness & flexibility

  • LLM‑agnostic platform: choose the best model for each task across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, NVIDIA, and open‑source options.
  • Support for skills and agents aligned to emerging industry standards, so Epsilon can reuse playbooks across providers.
  • Open APIs, MCP host and server support, and rich action libraries enable Epsilon to embed Glean context into existing AI tools (including PublicisGPT) instead of rebuilding plumbing.
  • Connectors and “Glean in” experiences for Slack, Teams, Salesforce, ServiceNow, Zendesk, and more ensure AI shows up where teams already work.
